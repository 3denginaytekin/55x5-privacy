Privacy Policy

55x5  ·  Last updated: May 18, 2026

55x5 ("the App") is developed by Engin Aytekin. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

Data Stored on Your Device

55x5 stores the following data locally on your device only:

- Your affirmation text
- Daily tap counts and cycle progress
- Completed cycle history
- Purchase unlock status

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server. Clearing the app's data or uninstalling the App permanently removes it.

Purchases

55x5 offers an optional one-time unlock purchase processed through Apple App Store and Google Play. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple or Google — we never see or store your payment details.

Purchase verification is powered by RevenueCat. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase data to verify entitlements. Please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

No Accounts Required

55x5 does not require you to create an account. No email address, name, or any personal identifier is collected.

No Third-Party Advertising

The App contains no advertising and no advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

55x5 is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be reflected with an updated date above.
